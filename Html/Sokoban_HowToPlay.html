How to Play

Sokoban is a strategy game where the goal is to push all the crates into the spots with red dots.
with the least possible moves in the shortest time possible. The crates will turn red when pushed onto a
tile with a red dot.


The gray tiles are unpassable and the user cannot move into those. In addition, you cannot move two
or more boxes in the same direction, as seen below.
